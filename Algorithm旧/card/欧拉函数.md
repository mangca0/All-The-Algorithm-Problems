# 性质
- 若n为质数，$\phi(n) = n - 1$
- 若n, m互质，即gcd(n, m) = 1，$\phi(n \cdot m) = \phi(n) \cdot \phi(m)$
- 若$n = p^k$，$\phi(n) = n \cdot (1 - \frac{1}{p})$
# 求$\phi(n)$
唯一分解定理：$n = {p1}^{k1} \cdot {p2}^{k2} \cdot ... \cdot {pm}^{km}$
$\phi(n) = n \cdot (1 - \frac{1}{p1}) \cdot (1 - \frac{1}{p2}) \cdot ... \cdot (1 - \frac{1}{pm})$
O()，取决于n的质因数个数
